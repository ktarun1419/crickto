Steps to follow to Buy CRICKTO on PancakeSwap

Here is the detailed step-by-step process you were waiting for.

Step 1: Create a MetaMask wallet.

You can access the MetaMask crypto wallet through a browser extension or mobile app. After downloading either of them through their official website, you can create a new wallet by carefully choosing a good seed phrase in addition to a strong password. Losing your seed phrase means losing all your funds inside the wallet, so exercise extra caution.

1. Install MetaMask extension (in browser) by heading over to metamask.io

2. Tap on the MetaMask extension and select “Get Started”. We are trying to create a new wallet here. But if you have a wallet already, you can easily import it using your seed phrase.

3. Tap on “Create Wallet”, and a new window will open. Here, MetaMask asks its users whether they agree to share their usage data to improve user experience. If you have no problem with that, you can click on “I agree”.

4. Now, you have to set a password for your crypto wallet. Create a “strong” password of a minimum of eight characters, a combination of alphabets, numerical and special characters. You can use a password manager to make your task easier.

5. The next step is crucial. You have to store the seed phrase of your wallet securely. Tap on “Click here to reveal secret words” to get the seed phrase. As We told you earlier, the seed phrase is the only way to recover your funds from the wallet, so better write it on a piece of paper and store it in a place where only you have access. Don’t trust your desktop notepad here. Also, you should never share these “secret words” with any website, app, or person unless you are willing to share the funds stored in it with them. Mind you, your wallet seed phrase is more important than your password.

6. Select “Next”, and in the new window, type each word of your seed phrase in the order in which they appeared in the last screen for confirmation. After it’s done, select “Confirm”.

7. You will get confirmation that your MetaMask wallet is created successfully with a party popper emoji. You can now access your wallet anytime by tapping the MetaMask icon on the top-right corner of your browser.

What is PancakeSwap?

PancakeSwap is a decentralized cryptocurrency exchange that allows you to trade or “swap” digital assets in crypto, precisely BEP-20 tokens. It’s built on Binance Smart Chain and competes against existing crypto giants like Uniswap and uses the Automated Market Maker Model (AMM).

Inside PancakeSwap, users have to create liquidity pools that will eventually help in the swapping of crypto. The platform offers you various DeFi farming opportunities to create user-generated liquidity pools. Liquidity pools break the traditional marketplace concept of buyers and sellers and create a collection of funds in a smart contract. The benefit of liquidity pools is that you can automatically trade digital assets without asking for permission from any centralized agency.

PancakeSwap has the most extensive user base among all decentralized apps, thanks to its low fees, better liquidity, and diverse staking options.
