NFT Marketplace

NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) are unique and unrepeatable tokens that exist thanks to a technology known as Blockchain.

In our platform Crickto, this token format is available for our community, where users can save up and sell sports event cards in NFT token format.

NFTs will be saved in their own wallets, which means that no matter what, they will be saved from the wrong hands.

P2P Betting

This feature consists in predicting the future rate of cryptocurrencies. This means that bettors can select any cryptocurrency and bet on the future price.

Bets will be established based on a period of time and choose if the price of a cryptocurrency will go up or down. For example, a week, a month, a day, hours.

This is a standard arbitrage operation in a decentralized platform. It may become one of the best ways to earn in Crickto.

Prediction Marketplace

This is a "yes or no" format bet, known as a betting binary format.

It consists of gamblers placing their bets on an upcoming event. For example, predicting a presidential election; if Apple will continue to be one of the best companies in the world. If the bettor's prediction was correct, the win paid will come instantly.

The odds for these predictions are based on the community.

P2E Games Betting

This is a "Play-to-Earn" betting format, where our platform gives the opportunity to bettors who want to earn from blockchain games.

In this betting game format, Crickto accepts the bets in every currency, from CRICKTO to Ethereum or any other crypto. This brings a new level of gambler interactions and creates a new economic model.

These games are a fit for all kinds of players, from the most experienced who know how to analyze the course of the game to those who only play for the adrenaline rush of winning if they feel lucky.

4. Select the crypto you want to exchange for CRICKTO. It may be BNB, USDT, etc.
5. Enter the amounts you want to exchange and swap them.
6. Pay the BSC chain gas fee and receive your tokens.

To get your tokens on Kanga, follow these instructions:

1. Go to kanga.exchange
2. Access your account. If you don’t have one, register.
3. Once you enter the platform, fund your Kanga wallet with the USDT amount you want to exchange.
4. Go to the “market” section and find the CRICKTO token in the “Find market” field.
5. Select it and complete the trade.
6. Make a withdrawal to your decentralized wallet.

After you have CRICKTO in your personal wallet, you can connect automatically to the Crickto platform and start playing.
