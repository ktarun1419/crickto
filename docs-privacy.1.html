Privacy policy

Welcome to Crickto.io, one of its main priorities is the privacy of participants who are visitors of Crickto.io and its dApps (the "Interface"). Crickto respects your right to privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. Crickto decides "why" and "how" your Personal Data is processed in connection with the Interface. If you have additional questions or require more information about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact team@crickto.io.
This Privacy Policy applies only to the Interface activities and the participants visiting the Interface with regards to the Personal Data they share and/or which is collected within Crickto. This Privacy Policy is not about Personal Data collected offline or via other channels. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your data and its treatment.

Changes to this agreement

If our data processing practices change, we will update this Privacy Policy. Thus you will beware of the updates first, which will give you a possibility to consent, object to a particular processing, or undertake any other action you are entitled to. Please keep track of any changes we may introduce to this Privacy Policy. Your continued access to and the use of the Interface proves you are aware of all amendments made to this Privacy Policy as of the date of your access and use of the Interface. So, we ask you to review the policy regularly for the reasons noted. If you are not satisfied with our personal data processing, you may immediately stop using the Interface.

Eligibility

To access or use the Interface, you must be able to form a legally binding contract with us. Accordingly, you declare that you are at least eighteen (18) years old. If you are under the age of eighteen (18), you may not, under any circumstances or for any reason, use the Interface.

Applicability

This Privacy Policy applies to all your interactions with us via the Interface and your interactions with us in connection therewith. Below are the categories of our processors which can access and process your Personal Data through the Interface:

  • Technical maintenance vendors;
  • Project and team management vendors;
  • Communication vendors;
  • Analytics, statistics, performance, marketing vendors;

Data processing in connection with the Interface

The data we collect about you Crickto tries to collect as minimum Personal Data from you as possible. We may use, store and transfer different kinds of Personal Data about you which we have grouped together follows:

  • Contact Data includes address, email address and telephone numbers;
  • Technical Data includes IP address, MAC address, log files, data related to usage, domain server, performance, website security, traffic patterns, location information, browser and device information. We only collect this type of data when you are using the Interface;
  • Financial Data includes wallet addresses, transaction, and balance information (blockchain data) that is accessible when interacting with the Interface;

In no event we are going to ask you to share your private keys or wallet seed. Never trust anyone or any website that asks you to enter your private keys or wallet seed.

How is your personal data collected?

We use different methods to collect data from and about you including through:

  • Direct interactions. You may give us your Identity, Contact requirements by completing the forms for our whitelist process;
  • Automated technologies or interactions. As you interact with our platform, we may automatically collect Technical Data by using cookies and other similar technologies. Please see our cookie policy or further details;
  • Third parties or publicly available sources. We may receive personal data about you from various third parties;
Why we use your Personal Data

We may use your Personal Data listed above only for:

  • Our internal and operational purposes, when: ensuring security, identifying irregular website behavior, preventing fraudulent activity and improving security at all possible levels;
  • Assessing and improving the performance of the Interface;
  • Analyzing our website visitors’ actions to improve our Interface (section "Cookies and Automatically Collected Data");
  • Analyzing the Interface behavior, including via: Google Analytics (please refer to Google's Analytics' Policy for more information);
  • Send you marketing, advertising, informational emails;
  • Find and prevent fraud;
Data retention period

Crickto will only retain your Personal Data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. In most cases we will aim to keep records of personal data for a minimum of 30 days after the expiry of our contract.

Cookies and Automatically Collected Data

Crickto uses cookies and similar tools to enhance your user experience, provide our services, and understand how to improve the Interface and interaction with it. The cookie banner on your browser will tell you how to accept or refuse cookies. The information we collect automatically may also include statistical and performance information arising from your use of the Interface. You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. To get more detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers, please find it on the browsers' respective websites.

Privacy of children

Crickto is not intended for children and we do not knowingly collect data relating to children. If you believe your child may have provided us with their data, you can contact us using the information in this Policy and we will delete the data from our Interface.

International Data Transferring

All information processed by us may be transferred, processed, and stored anywhere in the world, including but not limited to other countries, which may have data protection laws that are different from the laws where you live. We endeavor to safeguard your information consistent with the requirements of applicable laws.

Data Integrity & Security of Processing

Crickto designs systems with your security and privacy in mind. We work to protect the security of your personal information during transmission by using encryption protocols and software.

Contact information

Our data protection officer can be contacted at team@crickto.io , and will work to address any questions or issues that you have with respect to the collection and processing of your personal information.

Updates to this Privacy Policy

As noted, Crickto.io may review, change and update this Privacy Policy from time to time reflect our current practices and obligations. We will publish our current Privacy Policy on our website at Crickto.io and the changes will take effect at the time of publishing. You should review this privacy policy regularly and remain familiar with its terms.
